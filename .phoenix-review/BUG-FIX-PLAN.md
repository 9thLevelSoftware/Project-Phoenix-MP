# Bug Fix Plan

## Prioritization approach

- Quick wins: low-effort, high-impact items that can be fixed locally with guards, validation, clamping, or small state resets.
- Critical path: high-impact items that affect auth, data integrity, crashes, sync correctness, or release blockers and may require broader refactors.
- Technical debt: lower-impact or broader cleanup items that can be deferred after the release blockers are handled.
