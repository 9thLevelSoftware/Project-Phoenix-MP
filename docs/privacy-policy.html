Privacy Policy

Project Phoenix - Vitruvian Trainer Companion App

Effective Date: December 21, 2025  ·  Last Updated: June 5, 2026

Summary: Project Phoenix is a privacy-focused, offline-first app. Everything works on your device with no account required. Cloud sync and online sign-in are optional — if you choose to enable them, only the workout data you decide to sync is sent to your own private account. We never sell your data, and we don't use ads, analytics, or tracking SDKs.

1. Introduction

Project Phoenix ("we," "our," or "the App") is a community-developed companion application for Vitruvian Trainer fitness equipment. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the App accesses, how it is used, and your rights regarding your data. It covers both the mobile apps (Android and iOS) and the optional Phoenix Portal web companion.

2. Information We Collect

By default, the App stores all data locally on your device. The App does not require an account and does not transmit your data anywhere unless you explicitly enable optional features such as Cloud Sync or third-party integrations (see Sections 5 and 6).

The App stores the following data, primarily on your device:

  • Workout Data: Exercise sessions, sets, reps, weights, and timestamps
  • Performance Metrics: Real-time machine data during workouts (position, velocity, load, power)
  • Personal Records: Your best lifts and calculated one-rep max estimates
  • Custom Routines & Cycles: Workout routines and training programs you create
  • App Preferences: Settings such as weight unit (kg/lb), language, theme, and display options
  • Gamification Data: Badges earned, streaks, and achievement progress
  • Account Information (only if you sign in): If you enable Cloud Sync, an account is created using your email or a Google/Apple sign-in. We store the minimum needed to authenticate you and associate your synced data with your account.

3. Bluetooth Permissions

The App requires Bluetooth Low Energy (BLE) permissions to connect to your Vitruvian Trainer equipment. This connection is used exclusively for:

  • Discovering and connecting to your Vitruvian machine
  • Sending workout commands (weight, mode settings)
  • Receiving real-time workout metrics from the machine

Bluetooth data is processed in real-time and relevant metrics are stored on your device. Bluetooth data is never transmitted to any external party.

4. Optional Cloud Sync & Accounts

Cloud Sync and the Phoenix Portal web companion (an optional premium service at phoenix-portal.com) are opt-in. The App is fully functional without them. If you choose to enable Cloud Sync:

  • Account required for sync only: You create an account (email/password) or sign in with Google or Apple. You can use the entire app without ever creating one.
  • What is synced: Your workout sessions, routines, personal records, and related training data are uploaded so they can be accessed across your devices and in the Phoenix Portal web companion.
  • Where it is stored: Synced data is stored in our backend infrastructure (Supabase) under your account, protected by per-user access controls so only you can read your data.
  • Secure tokens: Authentication tokens are stored securely on your device (Android Keystore-backed storage / iOS Keychain).
  • You stay in control: You can use the app entirely offline, and you can request deletion of your synced data at any time (see Section 7).

5. Third-Party Services

The App does not use advertising networks, analytics SDKs, crash-reporting services, or social-media tracking SDKs. The only external services involved are ones that directly deliver features you choose to use:

  • Supabase — backend for optional Cloud Sync, accounts, and the Phoenix Portal. Only used if you enable Cloud Sync.
  • Google / Apple Sign-In — optional authentication providers, used only if you choose them to sign in.
  • Apple Health / Health Connect — optional. If you enable it, completed workouts can be written to your device's health platform under permissions you grant.
  • CSV Import/Export (Strong, Hevy formats) — file-based and fully under your control; no automatic transmission to those companies.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data, and we do not share it with third parties for advertising or marketing. Data you keep local stays local. Data you choose to sync is stored in your private account solely to provide the sync and Portal features to you. We only disclose data if required by law.

7. Your Rights and Data Control

You have complete control over your data:

  • Access: All your data is visible within the App's history and analytics screens
  • Deletion (local): Delete individual workouts and routines in-app, or clear all App data through your device settings
  • Deletion (synced): If you use Cloud Sync, you can request deletion of your account and synced data via the contact methods in Section 11
  • Portability: Export your workout data to CSV at any time, and back up locally using your device's standard backup mechanisms

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is designed for adult fitness enthusiasts using Vitruvian Trainer equipment.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated effective date. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.
