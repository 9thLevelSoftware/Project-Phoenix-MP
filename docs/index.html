Project Phoenix focuses on the training experience: fast Bluetooth pairing, reliable resistance control, flexible workout modes, detailed history, and safety-first controls that stay available even when you train without an internet connection.

Build routines, launch freestyle sessions, track every rep, and review progress across mobile and the optional Phoenix Portal. The app is free on Android and iOS, requires no account, shows no ads, and keeps the core workout flow local by default.

Everything it does

A complete trainer in your pocket

Far more than a remote control — Project Phoenix is a full strength-training platform that just happens to also drive your hardware.

Full machine control

Rock-solid Bluetooth connection with a hardened, reliability-tuned BLE engine and built-in diagnostics.

  • Set weight, mode & configuration from your phone
  • Motion-start detection — won't count until you move
  • Stall detection & automatic set end
  • Live BLE diagnostics & fault decoding

Real-time intelligence

Live load, velocity, position and power streamed straight from the machine, rep by rep.

  • Velocity-Based Training with live zones
  • Auto-end when bar speed drops off
  • Automatic, machine-accurate rep counting
  • Concentric / eccentric force breakdown

Build & program

A 572-exercise library and a routine builder powerful enough for real periodized programming.

  • Supersets with drag-and-drop & per-set rest
  • % of PR scaling & AMRAP sets
  • Multi-week training cycles
  • Routine folders & bulk weight adjust

Track everything

Automatic PRs, estimated 1RM and Smart Insights that actually understand your training history.

  • Phase- & mode-specific personal records
  • 1RM estimates (Brzycki / Epley)
  • Streaks, calendar & achievement badges
  • Smart Insights, trends & activity replay

Your data, your way

Local-first by design. Turn on optional cloud sync only if you want it — across phones and the web.

  • Optional cloud sync via Phoenix Portal (premium)
  • Multi-profile for shared households
  • Apple Health & Health Connect
  • CSV import/export (Strong & Hevy)

The "Safe Word" stop

A safety feature the original app never had: just say the word and the machine lets go.

  • Voice-activated emergency stop
  • Quick 3-rep calibration to your voice
  • Hands-free when a lift goes wrong
  • Works on Android & iOS

Questions

Good to know

Is the app really free?

Yes — the app is completely free: every feature, no ads, no in-app purchases. The only paid part is the optional web Phoenix Portal (cloud sync + dashboards), which you never need in order to use the app with your machine. Donations are welcome but always optional.

Which machines work with it?

The V-Form Trainer (VIT-200, 200 kg) and Trainer+ (220 kg / 110 kg per cable). The app connects to either over Bluetooth Low Energy.

Do I need an account or internet connection?

No. The app is fully functional offline with no account. Cloud sync via the premium Phoenix Portal and sign-in (Google/Apple) are entirely optional and only used if you turn them on.

Is my workout data private?

By default all data stays on your device. If you opt into cloud sync, only the data you choose to sync is sent to your private account. See the Privacy Policy for details.

Can I bring my data over from another app?

Yes — you can import and export workout data in Strong and Hevy CSV formats, and sync sessions to Apple Health or Health Connect.

Who builds Project Phoenix?

Project Phoenix is built by an independent community team focused on local control, workout programming, training history, and optional sync.
